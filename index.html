About

I am Hanzhe Li (李韩哲), a PhD student in Economics at the HKU Business School, The University of Hong Kong. My research interests lie in organizational economics, information economics, and the economics of AI.

Before joining HKU, I completed an M.Phil. in Economics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong and a bachelor's degree in Economics with a minor in Mathematics at Nanjing University. I have also been a visiting PhD student at Northwestern University and the University of Chicago.

My work studies organizations, incentives, and information design, including relational contracts, hierarchical organizations, and AI persuasion in professional settings.
